Mall Customer Data

Data Overview

This dataset contains information about mall customers, including demographics and spending patterns. The dataset has the following features:

  • CustomerID: Unique identifier for each customer
  • Gender: Customer's gender (Male/Female)
  • Age: Customer's age
  • Annual Income (k$): Customer's annual income in thousands of dollars
  • Spending Score (1-100): Score assigned by the mall based on customer behavior and spending patterns
